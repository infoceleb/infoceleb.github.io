Biography

Lauri Hendler was born in Virginia on April 22, 1965. Most well known for her portrayal of Julie Kanisky on the NBC sitcom Gimme a Break!, Hendler is also recognized for playing Hillary Flanagan on the ABC sitcom A New Kind of Family and for voicing Lindsay ‘Payback’ Price and Maya McEaddens on the USA Network animated series Wing Commander Academy. She appeared alongside Michael J Fox in the 1983 made-for-TV movie High School U.S.A. She launched her television acting career in the late 1970s, initially appearing in episodes of CHiPs, Lou Grant, and Three’s Company.
