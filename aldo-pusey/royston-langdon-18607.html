Biography

Royston Langdon was born in England on May 1, 1972. Famous as the frontman and bass player for the British rock group Spacehog, he is equally well known as the former husband of actress Liv Tyler. He and Sweeney Todd guitarist Nick Gilder both performed with popular British glam rock bands. In the mid-1990s, after performing together in the Leeds Parish Church choir, he and his brother Antony co-founded Spacehog.
