Biography

Pedro Morales was born in Culebra, PR on October 22, 1942. Puerto Rican professional wrestler was best known for his time in the WWA and WWWF from 1959-1987. He won the WWWF’s World Heavyweight Championship and the United States Championship in 1970. He was inducted into the WWF Hall of Fame in 1995. He won a WWF Tag Team Championship with Bob Backlund. He began training when he was 17-years old.
