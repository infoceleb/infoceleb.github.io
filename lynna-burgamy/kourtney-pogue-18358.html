Kourtney Pogue- Biography

Kourtney Pogue is a Chesterton, Indiana-born American actress, businesswoman, and former Los Angeles Kings ice girl. She rose to prominence after acting in the TV comedy film “Not Another High School Show.”

This celebrity starred in the film Bikini Party Girl, which was released on September 21, 2007. She had the opportunity to collaborate with the film’s director, Joel Gallen.

Eduardo- Birth, Age, Ethnicity, Siblings, Education

Kourtney was born in Chesterton, Indiana, on October 31, 1986. Her Zodiac sign is Scorpio. Her father’s name is Anthony Pogue, and her mother’s name is Tina McDonald Pogue.

Pogue then went on to Chesterton High School, where she graduated as one of the best students, and the University of California, Los Angeles, where she studied psychology. She completed her schooling in 2013.
