Biography

Bruce Li was born in Taiwan on June 5, 1950. Most well known for his title role in the 1976 martial arts movie Bruce Lee: The Man, The Myth, he also portrayed Bruce Lee in Bruce Lee: A Dragon Story (1974); Bruce Lee’s Secret (1976); Fists of Bruce Lee (1978); and over thirty other films. Li and Chinese actor and bodybuilder Bolo Yeung were both in the cast of the 1978 film The Image of Bruce Lee. Prior to Bruce Lee’s death and the introduction of the “Bruceploitation” movie genre, Li (then known as James Ho) performed film stunts in Hong Kong and Taiwan.
