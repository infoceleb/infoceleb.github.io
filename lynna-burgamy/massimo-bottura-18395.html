Biography

Massimo Bottura was born in Modena, Italy on September 30, 1962. Italian chef and restaurateur known for being the patron of Osteria Francescana, a three-Michelin-star restaurant that is based in his hometown of Modena. He is also known for having been profiled in the 2015 Netflix original series Chef’s Table. His restaurant was featured in the second season of Master of None after he approached Eric Wareheim and Aziz Ansari and asked them to be guests at his restaurant. He was inspired by his mother, aunt, and grandmother to pursue a career in cooking. He first began as an apprentice to Georges Coigny. After working on the restaurant Le Louis XV, he went on to open Osteria Francescana in March of 1995.
