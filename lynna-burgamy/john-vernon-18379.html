Biography

John Vernon was born in Canada on February 24, 1932. Star of Canadian television who played the humorless Dean Wormer in Animal House and portrayed the mayor of San Francisco in Dirty Harry. He also played a villain in Killer Klowns from Outer Space. He spoofed his common villainous roles in the Keenen Ivory Wayans blaxploitation spoof, I’m Gonna Git You Sucka. He starred in the CBC dramas A Cheap Bunch of Nice Flowers and Uncle Vanya before moving to the U.S. for film roles.
