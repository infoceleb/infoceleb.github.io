Biography

Helen Cornelius was born in Monroe City, MO on December 6, 1941. American country singer and actress who is best known for her duets with Jim Ed Brown in the 1970s and 1980s, including “I Don’t Want to Have to Marry You.” She recorded a 1978 version of the song “You Don’t Bring Me Flowers,” which is a song Barbra Streisand also recorded. She started a singing trio with her two sisters when she was young and they played local shows before she went solo. She later joined a band called The Crossroads.
