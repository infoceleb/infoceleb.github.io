Career

A politician from the United States who is now the mayor of Inglewood, California. During the 1970s and 1980s, he ascended through the ranks of law enforcement in Inglewood, eventually becoming a Deputy Chief. From 1991 to 2006, he was the Chief of Police in Santa Monica, California. In 2006, Butts joined Los Angeles World Airports as a public safety officer. In 2010, he was elected mayor of Inglewood, and in 2014, he was re-elected with an 84 percent vote. He oversaw the renovation and reopening of The Forum, as well as the development of a concept for SoFi Stadium in Hollywood Park.
