Biography

Murphy Lee was born in St. Louis, MO on December 18, 1978. Born Torhi Harper, he is a rapper and founding member, with Nelly, of the St. Lunatics hip hop group. He won an Grammy in 2004 for his participation on the song “Shake Ya Tailfeather.” His Grammy Award winning song “Shake Your Tailfeather,” featured Nelly and Diddy. As part of the St. Lunatics, he had a local hit with Gimme What U Got, and promoted Nelly to sign the first record deal, before he brought the rest of the group aboard.
