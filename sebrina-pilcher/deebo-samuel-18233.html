Deebo Samuel- Biography

Deebo Samuel is an American football wide receiver who now plays for the National Football League’s San Francisco 49ers (NFL). He previously played college football at South Carolina and was selected in the second round of the 2019 NFL Draft by the San Francisco 49ers. He holds the record for most running touchdowns by a wide receiver in a single season (2021) in NFL history.
