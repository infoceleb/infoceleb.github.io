Biography

Ron Killings was born in Atlanta, GA on January 19, 1972. American wrestler known under the ring names R-Truth and K-Kwik. He won a TNA World Tag Team Championship with Adam Jones in 2007 and a WWE Tag Team Championship alongside Kofi Kingston in 2012. He defeated Mike Mizanin, AKA The Miz, to win the WWE United States Championship in 2010. He began wrestling with the National Wrestling Alliance, an independent company, at age 25.
