Biography

Leigh Cappillino was born in South Carolina on April 18, 1969. With her Christian music quartet-turned-trio, Point of Grace, she earned numerous Dove Awards, including a Song of the Year honor for “How You Live (Turn Up The Music)” and an Album of the Year prize for No Changin’ Us. She and Texas-born vocalist Heather Payne were both members of Point of Grace. She attended both Anderson College and Charleston Southern University, earning degrees in, respectively, voice and religion.
