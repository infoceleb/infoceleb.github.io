Marcus Ericsson- Professional Career

At the 2009 Young Driver Test, Ericsson drove the Brawn BGP 001. Ericsson joined Caterham in 2014 as a driver. The team went into insolvency after the Russian Grand Prix, and Ericsson signed with Sauber for the 2015 season.

Ericsson began his second season with Sauber by finishing eighth in the season-opening Australian Grand Prix. He continued to score points, finishing the 2015 season in 18th place with 9 points.
