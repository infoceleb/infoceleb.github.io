Biography

Jim Ward was born in New York on May 19, 1959. The American voice actor won a 2009 Daytime Emmy Award for his work in an animated series called Biker Mice from Mars. He also voiced characters in Toy Story 3, Ben 10, and Evil Dead: Regeneration. On Nickelodeon’s The Fairly OddParents, he voiced several characters including Chet Ubetcha, Mr. Bickles, and Doug Dimmadome. He replaced fellow voice actor Peter Strauss as the voice of Stoker in the 1990s show Biker Mice from Mars. He attended the University of California, Los Angeles.
