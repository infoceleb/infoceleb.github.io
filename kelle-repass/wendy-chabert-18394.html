Wendy Chabert-Biography

Wendy Chabert is best known as the award-winning actress Lacey Chabert’s sister.

Her sister verified the heartbreaking news on her Instagram account. Many musicians also expressed their condolences to her family.

Wendy Chabert, Was She Killed In A Car Accident? – So, what happened?

Wendy Chabert, Lacey Chabert’s sister, died. However, the cause is unknown because neither her relatives nor anybody else has said anything. Nonetheless, speculations say that an automobile collision is to blame.

On October 24, 2021, her sister reported her death via her official Instagram account, @thereallacey. She then published another piece on Thanksgiving. And now, two days later, on the occasion of the premiere of Christmas at Castle Hart, she wrote that the loss had crushed her heart.

Wendy, who was of Cajun (French), Italian, English, and Scottish descent, died at the age of 46. She was the daughter of Julie (Johnson) and Tony Chabert, a salesperson for the oil business.

Aside from her parents, she has two additional sisters, TJ and Chrissy, in addition to Lacey.

She shared a home in Texas with her husband, Shane Riggio, and their two sons. Her marriage life, on the other hand, was presumably not going well because she was going through divorce proceedings. She filed for divorce in August 2019, and the case is still pending. She also filed two others in 2010 and 2017, both of which were dismissed.

Wendy, unlike Lacey, led a very private existence. We know very little about her. According to rumors, she used to work as a baker.

Her funeral arrangements are yet unclear. Hopefully, her soul rests in peace, and her family finds the fortitude to get through this difficult time.

Chabert, Wendy Sister

Lacey Chabert, an American actress, is Wendy Chabert’s elder sister.

Her sister has had a great acting career, having appeared in over 150 films and winning six Oscars in all.

Lacey won the IFS Award for Best Supporting Actress in 2018 and the Stinker Award for Worst Supporting Actress in 1998. She also received two Young Star Awards and a Young Artist Award for her performance in Party of Five. Similarly, she won an MTV Movie Award in 2005.

In terms of her personal life, she is a married woman. Her husband’s name is David Nehdar, and they married on December 22, 2013, in Los Angeles. Julia Mimi Bella Nehdar is the couple’s only child.
