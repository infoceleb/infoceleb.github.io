Shoni Schimmel- Biography

Shoni Schimmel was born in Oregon on May 4, 1992. Professional women’s basketball point guard who was named MVP of the 2014 WNBA All-Star game as a member of the Atlanta Dream. She won a gold medal representing the United States at the World University Games. She and center Stefanie Dolson were both taken in the first round of the 2014 WNBA Draft. She was a phenom at the University of Louisville, twice being named to the All-Big East Conference First Team.
