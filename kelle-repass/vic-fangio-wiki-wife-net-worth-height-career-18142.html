How did Vic Fangio start his Professional Career?

In 1975, Fangio played defensive back for Dunmore High. He served as the defensive coordinator and linebackers coach at his high school from 1979 to 1981.

He oversaw Milford Academy’s defense in 1982. In 1984, he then joined the Philadelphia/Baltimore Stars as a defensive assistant coach. From 1986 until 1994, Fangio coached the linebackers for New Orleans. He led the famed “Dome Patrol” during that time.

The Saints finished in the Top 5 in all four categories during Fangio’s nine seasons in New Orleans: overall defense (300.0 ypg, fifth), sacks (408, third), scoring defense (18.1 ppg, fourth), and takeaways (336 – T-3rd).
