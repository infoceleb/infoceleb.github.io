Biography

Luz Maria Doria was born in Cartagena, Colombia on July 10, 1965. Television executive and producer who is known for her work on the morning program Despierta America on the Univision network. She is also a columnist who is known for writing a weekly section in the Spanish newspapers La Opinion in Los Angeles and El Diario de NY in New York. She has worked directly with Alan Tacher, Karla Martínez, and Francisca Lachapel as part of the Despierta America program. She studied communications at Barry University in Miami, Florida where she graduated in 1986. She went on to intern at El Universal de Cartagena in Colombia. She became editor in chief of Cristina the Magazine in 1991 and went on to produce Despierta America in January of 2012.
